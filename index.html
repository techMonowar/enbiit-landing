Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need coding skills?
Absolutely not. Enbiit is designed as a No-Code platform. You use a visual builder to drag and drop elements. However, we do offer advanced features like Webhooks and JSON editing for developers who want more control.
Can I connect multiple Instagram accounts?
Yes! On the Pro and Business plans, you can connect multiple social media profiles and manage them from a single dashboard.
Is my data secure?
Security is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption for credentials (API Keys, Tokens) and do not store your customer data longer than necessary for processing. We are compliant with GDPR basics.
What happens if I hit my automation limit?
Your workflows will pause, and we will notify you immediately. You can choose to upgrade your plan or wait for the next billing cycle. We never charge overage fees without your permission.
